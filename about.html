Who Am I?

My name is Tim and my dream is to become a web developer! I have a naturally inquisitive mind so when something puzzles me, I have to know how it works. I get excited when making things from scratch, whether it was playing with lego as a kid, building an electric remote control car or making model airplanes.

There's limitless possibilities in software development and I want to explore the entire landscape of the industry, not just web. However, my main goal is to start with web dev.

I'm more of a jeans, tshirt and sneakers guy than a suit & tie. If it wasn't socially frowned upon, I'd go out in my pyjamas.

I'm laid-back and not overly competitive by nature. Winning for me is more about enjoying the moment for what it is and not about coming first.
